About Picturistic

Picturistic is a Pinterest-inspired image sharing platform. Users can discover, upload, and interact with beautiful images from around the world. Our mission is to make visual inspiration accessible and engaging for everyone.

  • Infinite scroll gallery
  • User authentication
  • Image upload and management
  • Like, comment, and view tracking
  • Responsive, modern design

Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and SQL.
